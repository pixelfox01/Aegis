# Introduction to Raven

Welcome to Raven, your privacy control platform. Raven gives you complete visibility and control over your data, terms and conditions, and privacy agreements.

## What is Raven?

Raven is a comprehensive privacy management platform that helps you:

- **Track Terms & Conditions**: See all the terms you've agreed to across different services
- **Manage Privacy Settings**: Control your privacy preferences in one central location
- **Data Visibility**: Understand what data companies have collected about you
- **Self-Hosting**: Full control by hosting on your own infrastructure
